The Old School Way

ABOUT THE EVENT

Do you miss sitting hours in front of your screen for playing game like Tetris, Mario, Contra etc especially in summer holidays?

Do you want to relive those days again and play these games along with coding?

GSDC RCOEM is bringing to you another fun, creative and innovative event which will make you visit your old childhood lanes.

What

It will be combination of multiple levels filled with questions, riddles, crosswords and Auctions which will be tech based as well as game based, aiming to enlighten the coder as well as the gamer within you.

How

It will be an offline event in which participants should divide themselves in group of 3 and each group will be filtered after each levels. Participants will try to collect as many points by solving all the questions.

When

The registration starts from 26th November.
